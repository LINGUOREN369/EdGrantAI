PROGRAM: Walmart Local Community Grants

SUMMARY:
The Walmart Local Community Grant program provides funding to local nonprofit organizations that support the needs of the communities Walmart serves.

FUNDING PRIORITIES:
• Hunger relief and healthy eating
• Health and human services
• Quality of life improvements
• Education and workforce readiness
• Community resilience and safety

AWARD AMOUNT:
$250 to $5,000 per organization

ELIGIBILITY:
• 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations, recognized churches, or recognized government entities
• Must operate in the local service area of the Walmart or Sam’s Club store where the grant is requested
• Must serve the community’s underserved or marginalized populations

GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS:
United States, local communities

EXCLUSIONS:
• Political organizations
• Individuals
• Capital campaigns
• Endowments
• Medical research

APPLICATION NOTES:
Applications are reviewed quarterly. Organizations may submit up to 25 applications per year across all locations.
